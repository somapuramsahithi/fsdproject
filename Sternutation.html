Sternutation

Definition: The formal medical term for sneezing—the involuntary expulsion of air through the nose and mouth due to nasal irritation.
Examples:
  • "A sudden sternutation erupted during the silent exam."
  • "The pollen storm caused multiple sternutations across the room."
  • "He covered his mouth mid‑sternutation, but the sound slipped out."
  • "Her sternutation startled the cat off the couch."
